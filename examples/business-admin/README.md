# Business/Admin Preset

For executives, assistants, business owners, and anyone handling a high volume of email, reporting, meetings, and administrative tasks. This preset configures Cowork to produce professional communications and structured outputs that are ready to send.

---

## Who this is for

Business professionals who spend significant time on email, reports, meeting preparation, and administrative tasks. Particularly useful for people who need Cowork to produce polished, stakeholder-ready outputs quickly with minimal revision.

---

## What this preset configures

- **Custom instructions:** Professional business tone, stakeholder-ready output, action-oriented communication
- **Skills:** Email drafting, report summary, action items
- **Folder structure:** Inbox/, Reports/, Emails/, Meetings/, Templates/
- **Connectors:** Gmail (strongly recommended), Google Drive (recommended)

---

## How to use this preset

1. Run the setup wizard (open WIZARD.md in Cowork and say "Help me set up")
2. Select "Business/Admin" when asked about your goal
3. Answer "What does a typical work day look like?" to calibrate the preset
4. Follow SETUP-CHECKLIST.md to complete configuration

---

## First session prompts to try

- "Draft a professional email declining a meeting request politely, keeping the relationship warm, in under 100 words. Then draft a version that's 30% more direct."
- "What files are in my Inbox/ folder? Draft a prioritized action list for today."
