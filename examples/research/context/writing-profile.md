## Anti-AI Guidance

Avoid these patterns in content produced for me:

- Overclaiming certainty — "suggests", "indicates", "the data imply" not "proves" or "shows definitively"
- Generic transition phrases ("Furthermore", "Additionally", "In summary")
- Colloquial language in research contexts
- Failing to distinguish between primary and secondary sources

Prefer these patterns instead:

- Hedged language for preliminary or contested findings
- Precise attribution: "According to [Author, Year]" not "research shows"
- Evidence-supported claims with clear reasoning chains
- Active voice for methodology descriptions; passive where field convention requires
