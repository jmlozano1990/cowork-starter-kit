# Research Preset

For academic researchers, analysts, and anyone conducting systematic literature review or evidence synthesis. This preset configures Cowork as a research partner that works with your source materials and maintains citation discipline.

---

## Who this is for

Graduate students, researchers, analysts, and professionals who spend significant time reading and synthesizing sources — PDFs, web articles, reports, or datasets. Designed for people who need structured outputs, clear attribution, and rigorous handling of evidence.

---

## What this preset configures

- **Custom instructions:** Academic/analytical tone, rigorous citation discipline, structured report format
- **Skills:** Literature review, source analysis, research synthesis
- **Folder structure:** Literature/, Notes/, Drafts/, Data/, References/
- **Connectors:** Google Drive (recommended for cloud-based source libraries)

---

## How to use this preset

1. Run the setup wizard (open WIZARD.md in Cowork and say "Help me set up")
2. Select "Research" when asked about your goal
3. Answer "Are your sources mostly PDFs, web pages, or both?" to calibrate output
4. Follow SETUP-CHECKLIST.md to complete configuration

---

## First session prompts to try

- "I'm starting a literature review on [your research topic]. What are the 5 most important questions I should be trying to answer, and what types of sources should I look for?"
- "Look at my Literature/ folder. What sources do I have and what topics do they cover?"
