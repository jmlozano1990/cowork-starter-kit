# Writing Preset

For authors, content creators, journalists, bloggers, and anyone who writes regularly. This preset configures Cowork to understand your voice, support your process, and help you produce content that sounds like you.

---

## Who this is for

Writers of any kind — blog posts, essays, fiction, reports, newsletters, journalism. Particularly useful for people who have a defined voice or style they want Cowork to match, and for writers who need help moving from rough ideas to polished drafts.

---

## What this preset configures

- **Custom instructions:** Voice-matching focus, minimal unsolicited suggestions, writing-process support
- **Skills:** Voice matching, editing pass, outline generator
- **Folder structure:** Drafts/, Published/, Ideas/, Research/, Voice-and-Style/
- **Connectors:** Google Drive (recommended if you draft in Docs)

---

## How to use this preset

1. Run the setup wizard (open WIZARD.md in Cowork and say "Help me set up")
2. Select "Writing" when asked about your goal
3. Answer "What type of content do you create most?" to calibrate the preset
4. Follow SETUP-CHECKLIST.md to complete configuration

---

## First session prompts to try

- "I need to write [type of content] about [any topic]. Give me 3 different opening paragraphs with different tones — formal, conversational, and punchy — so I can see which feels most like my voice."
- "Read my Voice-and-Style/ folder and write me a 150-word sample in my voice about [any topic]."
