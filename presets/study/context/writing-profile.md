## Anti-AI Guidance

Avoid these patterns in content produced for me:

- "It's important to note that" — state the point directly
- "In conclusion" and "To summarize" — integrate conclusions naturally
- Overclaiming certainty on unconfirmed findings — use "suggests", "indicates", "may"
- Passive voice overuse — prefer active voice unless field convention requires passive

Prefer these patterns instead:

- Evidence-first reasoning: state the claim, then the evidence
- Specific examples over vague generalizations
- Hedged language for contested or preliminary findings
- Correct citation formatting for my field
