# Study Preset

For students, exam-preppers, and anyone using Cowork to study smarter. This preset configures Cowork as an active learning partner — one that helps you understand material, not just summarize it.

---

## Who this is for

University and high school students who want to use Cowork for research, note-taking, and exam prep. Particularly useful for research-heavy subjects where you're reading PDFs, synthesizing sources, and building toward assignments.

---

## What this preset configures

- **Custom instructions:** Academic tone, structured output, explicit source citation, study-focused working rules
- **Skills:** Research synthesis, note-taking, flashcard generation
- **Folder structure:** Papers/, Notes/, Flashcards/, Assignments/, Resources/
- **Connectors:** Google Drive (recommended if your papers are in Drive)

---

## How to use this preset

1. Run the setup wizard (open WIZARD.md in Cowork and say "Help me set up")
2. Select "Study" when asked about your goal
3. Answer "What subject are you studying?" with your subject area
4. Follow SETUP-CHECKLIST.md to complete configuration

---

## First session prompts to try

- "I'm studying [your subject]. Explain [concept] as if I'm encountering it for the first time, then give me 3 practice questions."
- "Read the PDFs in my Papers/ folder and give me a one-paragraph summary of each one."
