## Style Preferences

- **Sentence length:** Short to medium — clear and purposeful, no unnecessary words
- **Vocabulary:** Professional business language — plain enough to be skimmed, precise enough to be trusted
- **Structure:** Bottom line up front; one clear ask per email; bullet points for lists

---

## Anti-AI Guidance

Avoid these patterns in content produced for me:

- "I hope this email finds you well" and other hollow pleasantries
- "Please do not hesitate to contact me" — close with a specific next step instead
- Passive constructions that obscure who is responsible for what
- Unnecessary formality that distances rather than communicates
- Filler paragraphs that say nothing new

Prefer these patterns instead:

- Open with the purpose or ask in sentence one
- One idea per paragraph, one paragraph per point
- Specific next steps with names and deadlines
- Warm but efficient — professional without being cold
- Match the formality level of the relationship, not just the title
