# Project Management Preset

For project managers, team leads, ops professionals, and anyone who manages multiple ongoing projects. This preset configures Cowork to produce structured status updates, meeting notes, and risk assessments in the format your stakeholders expect.

---

## Who this is for

PMs, program managers, team leads, and ops professionals who spend time in meetings, writing status reports, and tracking project health. Particularly useful for people managing multiple concurrent projects who want Cowork to help them stay organized and communicate clearly.

---

## What this preset configures

- **Custom instructions:** Professional PM tone, structured output format, stakeholder-ready communication
- **Skills:** Status updates, meeting notes, risk assessment
- **Folder structure:** Active-Projects/, Archive/, Templates/, Meeting-Notes/, Inbox/
- **Connectors:** Google Drive (strongly recommended), Gmail (recommended)

---

## How to use this preset

1. Run the setup wizard (open WIZARD.md in Cowork and say "Help me set up")
2. Select "Project Management" when asked about your goal
3. Answer "What tools does your team use?" to calibrate connector recommendations
4. Follow SETUP-CHECKLIST.md to complete configuration

---

## First session prompts to try

- "I'm managing a project to [describe any project]. What are the top 5 risks I should be tracking, and draft a one-paragraph status update I could send to a stakeholder today."
- "What's in my Active-Projects/ folder? Summarize the status of each project in 2 sentences."
