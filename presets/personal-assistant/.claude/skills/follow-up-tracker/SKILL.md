## Follow-Up Tracker

**When to use:** When you've mentioned a commitment, shared a conversation, or pasted inbox content that contains promises, next steps, or outstanding items from someone else.

**Instructions:** Read the provided content. Identify every explicit or implied commitment: (1) things the user owes (owner = user); (2) things others owe the user (owner = named person). For each: log who, what, and by when (if stated). Save to People/<name>.md or Tasks/ if no specific person. Flag items with no deadline as "no deadline set."

**Example prompts:**

- "I just got off a call with [name]. They said they'd send the contract by Friday. Track that."
- "Here's my inbox from this week: [paste]. What do I owe people? What do they owe me?"
- "Show me all open follow-ups from my People/ folder."
