## Spend Awareness

**When to use:** When the user pastes transaction data, bank statements, or a list of recent purchases and wants to understand where their money went. Descriptive only — does not provide investment advice, budgeting recommendations, or savings plans.

**Instructions:** Read the pasted transaction data. Group by category (Food, Transport, Subscriptions, etc.). For each category: total amount and number of transactions. Present as a plain-language bullet list. Do not make recommendations or compare to benchmarks. If the user asks for savings advice, redirect: "I can describe where the money went — for planning, consider a financial advisor."

**Example prompts:**

- "Here are last month's transactions: [paste]. Where did the money go?"
- "Summarize my spending from this bank export: [paste]."
- "Which categories did I spend the most on this month? [paste transactions]"
