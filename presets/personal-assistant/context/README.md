# Context Files — Personal Assistant Preset

This folder contains reference files that you share with Cowork to personalize your workspace. These files are not auto-loaded — you share them explicitly by saying "Here's my profile:" and pasting the content, or by pointing Cowork to the file.

---

## Files in this folder

| File | Purpose | How to use |
|------|---------|------------|
| `about-me.md` | Your name, role, schedule, and goals | Share at the start of a session to give Cowork context about who you are |
| `working-rules.md` | Your preferences for how Cowork should behave | Pre-filled with safe defaults — edit to match your style |
| `output-format.md` | Your preferred output style | Pre-filled for Personal Assistant work — tweak as needed |
| `writing-profile.md` | Your writing voice and tone | Used by Cowork when drafting messages or notes on your behalf |

---

## How to update these files

Open each file in a text editor (Notepad, TextEdit, or any app that saves plain text) and fill in the bracketed placeholders. Save the file and share it with Cowork when prompted.

---

## Privacy note

These files live on your local machine only. They are not synced anywhere unless you explicitly share them outside of Cowork. The `about-me.md` file may contain personal details — keep it in your local project folder and do not commit it to a shared repository.
