# Creative Preset

For designers, storytellers, creative strategists, and anyone working on creative projects. This preset configures Cowork as a generative creative partner — one that helps you explore, not just execute.

---

## Who this is for

Creative professionals who want a thinking partner for ideation, project development, and creative feedback. Useful whether you work solo or on a team, in any creative medium — design, writing, strategy, art, film, music, or other creative fields.

---

## What this preset configures

- **Custom instructions:** Generative and expansive tone, idea-first approach, minimal constraints on brainstorming
- **Skills:** Ideation, creative brief, feedback synthesis
- **Folder structure:** Projects/, Inspiration/, Drafts/, Assets/, Archive/
- **Connectors:** Google Drive (recommended if you store assets in Drive)

---

## How to use this preset

1. Run the setup wizard (open WIZARD.md in Cowork and say "Help me set up")
2. Select "Creative" when asked about your goal
3. Answer "Do you work solo or with a team?" to calibrate collaboration features
4. Follow SETUP-CHECKLIST.md to complete configuration

---

## First session prompts to try

- "I'm working on [describe any creative project]. Give me 5 unexpected directions I could take this — include at least one that surprises me."
- "Read my Inspiration/ folder and suggest 3 creative directions I could explore this week."
