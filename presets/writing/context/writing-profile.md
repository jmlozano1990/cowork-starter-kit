## Anti-AI Guidance

Avoid these patterns in content produced for me:

- "It's important to note" — state the point
- "Certainly!" and "Great question!" — respond without affirmations
- Passive voice overuse — especially in narrative contexts
- Clichéd transitions: "Furthermore", "In conclusion", "It goes without saying"
- Generic "as an AI" qualifiers and excessive hedging

Prefer these patterns instead:

- Active voice, first person where appropriate
- Specific details and examples over abstract generalizations
- Natural paragraph transitions, not signal-word transitions
- Sentence variety — mix short punchy sentences with longer ones
- Preserve idiosyncrasies — they are features of my voice, not errors
